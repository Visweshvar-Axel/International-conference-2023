How to reach?

By Air

  • Kannur International Airport is 98 km from the Central University of Kerala.
  • Mangalore International Airport is 82 km from the Central University of Kerala
  • Kozhokkode International Airport is 190 km from the Central University of Kerala

    • By Train

  • Kanhangad railway station is 12 km from the Central University of Kerala
  • Kasargod railway station is 22 km from the Central University of Kerala

    • By Road

  • The Central University of Kerala is situated on National Highway (NH 66) which connects Mumbai and Kanyakumari.
