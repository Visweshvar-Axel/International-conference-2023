Call for Abstract

Abstracts are invited for oral and poster presentations in the scientific sessions of the conference.


Submission of abstract

Abstract for oral and poster presentations should be typed in word (.doc or .docx) in Times New Roman 12 pt with maximum of 250 word counts and five keywords. Presenting author name should be underlined. The abstract can be submitted to icbrt@cukerala.ac.in


Publications

Presented papers will be reviewed and published in reputed journals or as book chapters in an edited book with ISBN by international publishers after the conference..
